# Wester | Opt-In
## tl;dr
Opt-In means that your data will be added to Wester's corpora. Contact joel@webrelics.xyz to make it happen!

## Steps
### Step 1 - Prepare your data:
#### Prepare your data
Get your data ready, a .txt file makes the process faster. Please make sure, that the prepared data is actually yours and not licensed data.
### Step 2 - Send your data:
Next, contact me - joel@webrelics.xyz - with your attached .txt file. Also add a short description on how this data may be useful to Wester's corpora.

## What now?
Your data will ether be added to the corpora or not. You'll be able to find it under /docs/yourData.txt
## What is not allowed?
Anything like Terms of Services, Privacy Policies, Imprints and such are generally not allowed. Repetetive data is also not allowed.
